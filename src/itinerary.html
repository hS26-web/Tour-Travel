Manali & Kasol Journey

4 Days | 3 Nights | Premium Group Experience

🗺️ Complete Itinerary

Day-by-Day Experience

A carefully curated blend of adventure, bonding, local exploration, and absolute relaxation.

Day 0 • Evening to Morning

Delhi → Manali

Overnight Road Journey

Board the comfortable Volvo bus from Kashmere Gate, Delhi at 6:30 PM. Meet your friendly trip coordinator and fellow travelers. Get ready for a scenic overnight journey through Himalayan highways as you head towards Manali.

Volvo Bus Overnight Group Intro
Day 1 • Morning to Evening

Manali Arrival & DJ Night

Old Manali Exploration

Arrive in Manali in the morning. After breakfast, explore Mall Road for local shopping and street food. Visit the ancient Hadimba Devi Temple surrounded by pine forests. Relax at cozy cafes in Old Manali. Evening features an exclusive DJ Night to dance and celebrate with your group.

Hadimba Temple DJ Night Local Cafes
Day 2 • Full Day Adventure

Atal Tunnel & Solang Valley

Snow Activities & Scenic Beauty

Drive through the engineering marvel - Atal Tunnel - into Sissu Valley. Take photos by the stunning waterfall and frozen landscapes. Proceed to Solang Valley for paragliding, zorbing, and outdoor winter activities (at personal cost) before returning to Manali for dinner.

Atal Tunnel Solang Valley Snow Fun
Day 3 • Full Day Camping

Kasol Camping & Bonfire

Riverside Camping Magic

Travel to the scenic Parvati Valley. Check into riverside luxury camps in Kasol surrounded by soaring pines. Explore the local Hippie Market and cross Chalal Bridge. As evening sets in, enjoy group games, a warm bonfire, and stargazing under the clear mountain skies.

Kasol Camp Bonfire Stargazing
Day 4 • Morning to Evening

Manikaran Sahib & Delhi Return

Sacred Pilgrimage & Departure

Wake up to the sound of Parvati River. Visit the sacred Manikaran Sahib Gurudwara and experience the natural hot springs. Explore local markets for unique souvenirs. Have lunch in Kasol, and board your evening Volvo back to Delhi with lifelong friends.

Hot Springs Gurudwara Departure
