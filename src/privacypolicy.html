Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

H & S Global Ventures respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit our website or enquire about our tours, and how we use and protect that information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you contact us or book a trip:

  • Name, phone number, and email address
  • Travel preferences, destination, dates, and group size
  • Payment-related details shared for booking (processed securely as applicable)
  • ID or permit details required for certain destinations
  • Messages sent through our website, phone, or email

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to enquiries and prepare travel quotes
  • To confirm bookings and share itinerary updates
  • To coordinate hotels, transport, and tour arrangements
  • To send important service notifications related to your trip
  • To improve our website and customer experience

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share necessary details only with trusted partners such as hotels, transport providers, and local operators to fulfil your booking. We may also disclose information if required by law or government authorities.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. However, no online transmission is 100% secure. Please avoid sharing sensitive payment details over unsecured channels.

5. Cookies & Website Usage

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to improve browsing, remember preferences, and understand visitor traffic. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work as expected.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as needed to provide services, meet legal requirements, or resolve disputes. Old enquiry records may be deleted periodically unless required for accounting or legal purposes.

7. Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete your personal information by contacting us. We will respond within a reasonable time, subject to legal and operational requirements.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites (maps, social media, payment gateways, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites. Please review their policies separately.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date. Continued use of our website or services means you accept the updated policy.
