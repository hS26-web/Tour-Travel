Delhi Departure

Delhi to Manali

Overnight Road Journey

Board the comfortable Volvo bus from Kashmere Gate, Delhi at 6:30 PM. Meet your friendly trip coordinator and fellow travelers. Get ready for a scenic overnight journey through Himalayan highways as you head towards Manali.

Volvo Bus Overnight Journey Group Intro
1
Arrival & Party

Manali Arrival & DJ Night

Sightseeing & Old Manali Vibe

Arrive in Manali in the morning. After breakfast, explore Mall Road for local shopping and street food. Visit the ancient, pine-forest surrounded Hadimba Devi Temple. Relax at cozy cafes in Old Manali. Evening features an exclusive DJ Night to dance and celebrate with your group.

Hadimba Temple Mall Road DJ Night
2
Snow & Valleys

Atal Tunnel & Solang Valley

Sissu Valley Sightseeing

Drive through the engineering marvel, Atal Tunnel, into Sissu Valley. Take photos by the stunning waterfall and frozen landscapes. Proceed to Solang Valley for paragliding, zorbing, and outdoor winter activities (at personal cost) before returning to Manali.

Atal Tunnel Sissu Valley Snow Fun
3
Camping Magic

Kasol Camping & Bonfire

Adventure Riverside Camping

Travel to the scenic Parvati Valley. Check into riverside luxury camps in Kasol surrounded by soaring pines. Explore the local Hippie Market and cross Chalal Bridge. As evening sets in, enjoy group games, a warm bonfire, and stargazing under the clear skies.

Kasol Camping Chalal Bridge Bonfire
4
Hot Springs & Return

Manikaran Sahib & Delhi Return

Peaceful Sightseeing

Wake up to the sound of Parvati River. Visit the sacred Manikaran Sahib Gurudwara and experience the natural hot springs. Explore local markets for unique souvenirs. Have lunch in Kasol, and board your evening Volvo back to Delhi with lifelong friends.
